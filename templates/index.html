Hackathon

In this hackathon, we have used AI to retrieve data from the energy consumption of specific machines in order to monitor their usage and calculate how much power each machine consumes. By doing so, we can analyze the energy consumption of the various machines, and AI can predict and measure when and for how long they need to be powered on.
